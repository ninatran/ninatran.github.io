BME 100: Introduction to Biomedical Engineering

Course description

Introduces engineering design methods, experimental practices, data gathering, presentation and statistical analysis. Lecture/labs focus on device design and application, including hands-on experience.

Prerequisites

Biomedical engineering major

Critical course

Yes
