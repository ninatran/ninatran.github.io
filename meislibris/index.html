It paints a bleak future. The common man dreams of owning his own live animal: a symbol of status in an age where animals have become mostly extinct. The man who cannot afford an animal may opt to buy an electric animal made of wires and mechanical parts dressed up in a skin to simulate the real thing. It asks the question: "What does it mean to be human?" Its answer is unsatisfying and dismal.
